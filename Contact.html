Marigold Composters is a trademark of Prudent Eco Systems Pvt. Ltd. based out of Bangalore, India. Prudent Eco Systems was founded in 2014 with the aim of providing high-quality composting solutions to individual homes and large apartment complexes. Today, Marigold Composters are used in over 30 large apartment associations for regularly composting kitchen waste. Marigold Composters are made with minimal plastic, and are designed and manufactured in India
